Overview

'Geometry of the Sentiments’ was an art exhibition held by 1a space from January to June 2024. As part of its marketing campaign, curator and artist interview videos were shared on Facebook, Instagram and YouTube to deepen audience’s interest in the exhibition. I played a vital role in the video production, involving in the entire process from planning to shooting and editing.

To ensure the interviews were engaging and informative, my colleague and I examined all the relevant materials related to the exhibition, curator, and artists. For each interview, we crafted 5 to 10 interview questions, designed to capture the audience's attention and offer them deeper insights. During the shooting process, I took charge of the filming equipment and operated the camera. Once we had gathered all the footage and exhibition photos, I took charge of audio mixing, video editing, and colour grading to enhance videos’ appeal.

The final result were full interview videos that we shared on YouTube, with each question displayed on the screen. This approach allowed the audience to first engage with the interview highlights on Facebook and Instagram, building curiosity. Subsequently, they could dive into the full YouTube video for a more comprehensive experience, gaining a profound understanding of the exhibition.
