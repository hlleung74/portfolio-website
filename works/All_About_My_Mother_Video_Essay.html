Overview

All About My Mother (1999) is a Spanish Oscar-winning melodrama-comedy by Pedro Almodóvar. This video essay dives into the interconnected relationships between characters, exploring gender-related topics, including gender roles and sexual orientations. Film footage and motion graphics, comprised of images that are either from Canva or prepared by my groupmates, are added to the video to make the content more digestible and engaging.
