Overview

While I was employed with the IT Team at Hong Kong Housing Society (HKHS), I created cybersecurity awareness training materials for more than 1400 employees. I used animated videos, comic strips, and quizzes to make complex security concepts approachable and applicable to everyday situations.

I worked closely with my team through iterative feedback cycles to guarantee relevancy and efficacy.
