Overview

As an anti-scam campaign student helper at the City University of Hong Kong, I designed social media content on scam awareness for the City-Youth Empowerment Project's (CYEP's) Instagram account. Due to my experiences at HKHS, I proposed spreading scam awareness through comic strips to the team.

On this project, I collaborated with my team members and a freelance artist. I was responsible for comic writing and storyboarding. After coming up with the storyline and drafting the storyboard of each comic chapter, I discussed them with my team during our meetings and amended the content based on their feedback. Then I would hand over my finalised storyboards with some remarks to the artist to draw the comic strips digitally.

This project was initially planned as an eight-part serialised Instagram comic, with the complete draft of storyboards. Unfortunately, with CYEP being discontinued after 2022, only the first two episodes were completed, which are as follows:
