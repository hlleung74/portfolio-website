Overview

'Geometry of the Sentiments’ was an art exhibition held by 1a space from January to June 2024. To generate anticipation and engage the audience, teaser videos were shared through Facebook and Instagram Reels prior to the exhibition's commencement. Taking on the responsibility of video production, I played a central role in the entire process, from planning to the final publishing of the videos.
