Overview

As a cultural partner of Art Basel Hong Kong 2024, 1a space showcased the ‘Geometry of Sentiments’ parallel exhibition at the Hong Kong Convention and Exhibition Centre. This exhibition coincided with a six-month-long exhibition of the same name hosted at 1a space's gallery. Recognizing the interconnected nature of these exhibitions and the potential unfamiliarity among visitors, I produced the video recap below, shedding light on the ongoing exhibition. This video was played on a TV inside 1a space’s exhibition booth, ensuring visitors gained a comprehensive understanding of the exhibition.

The photos and videos used in this video were captured by my colleagues and me. I planned the structure of the video, highlighting the content in chronological order to showcase the unfolding of events within the exhibition. With this plan in mind and the available footage, I edited this 5-minute video, curating a visual narrative that encapsulates the exhibition.
