Overview

As part of 1a space’s marketing team, I promoted art exhibitions and programmes via Instagram, Facebook, and email. My position comprised creating engaging and visually appealing content to attract in-person participation while keeping brand consistency across platforms and responding to stakeholder requirements.

Instagram & Facebook

Worked with colleagues, artists, and curators to create video and photographic content for 1a space’s social channels, including:

  • Exhibition/Programme Promotional Videos: Led the entire process of creating promotional videos, from narrative development to post-production editing. Content was finalised through team assessments to ensure alignment with the curatorial vision and audience engagement goals.
  • Artist/Curator Interview Highlights: Co-developed interview questions, coordinated shooting responsibilities (e.g., camera operation, lighting setup), and carried out post-production editing for interview clips. Streamlined feedback loops ensure curatorial vision and audience engagement.
  • Event Photography: Captured audience participation and panel discussions for social media marketing and archival purposes.
