Overview

As part of 1a space’s marketing team, I promoted art exhibitions and events across various channels, including social media (Facebook, Instagram and YouTube), the company website, and email newsletters. My position comprised creating engaging and visually appealing content to attract in-person participation while keeping brand consistency across platforms and responding to stakeholder requirements.

Social Media (Facebook, Instagram and YouTube)

Worked with colleagues, artists, and curators to create video and photographic content for 1a space’s social channels, including:

  • Artist/Curator Interview: Co-developed interview questions, coordinated shooting responsibilities (e.g., camera operation, lighting setup), and carried out post-production editing for interview clips. Streamlined feedback loops ensure curatorial vision and audience engagement.
  • Exhibition/Programme Promotional Videos: Led the entire process of creating promotional videos, from narrative development to post-production editing. Content was finalised through team assessments to ensure alignment with the curatorial vision and audience engagement goals.
  • Event Photography: Captured audience participation and panel discussions for social media marketing and archival purposes.
