Overview

As a security team’s IT intern at Hong Kong Housing Society (HKHS), one of my duties was to create cybersecurity awareness training materials for internal staff to protect the organization from cyber-attacks. I worked on this monthly comics project under the supervision of my manager. Multiple platforms are involved in the creation of this project, including Pixton and Canva. After coming up with the topics and drafting the comics, I would send them to my manager for review and amend the content based on his feedback. Upon completion, a comic strip would be emailed to the internal staff at the end of the month.

This project focuses on topics ranging from QR code scams to CEO fraud. Some of the comic examples are selected and presented as follows:
